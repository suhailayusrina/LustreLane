What is Lustre Lane? ▼

Lustre Lane suggests a path or journey that leads to elegance, shine, or beauty. "Lustre" refers to a soft glow or sheen, often associated with polished surfaces like gemstones or jewelry, which emphasizes the luxurious and refined nature of the products. "Lane" evokes the idea of a distinct, exclusive space, inviting visitors to explore and experience something beautiful. Together, "Lustre Lane" could symbolize a place where beauty and sophistication meet, guiding people toward the world of fine, radiant jewelry.

What are the material of your jewellery? Is it hypoallergenic? ▼

Our jewelry is crafted using high-quality materials such as sterling silver, 14k and 18k gold, stainless steel, and occasionally plated metals. We also incorporate gemstones, crystals, and pearls, both natural and cultured. Most of our designs are hypoallergenic, as we prioritize using nickel-free and lead-free materials, making them suitable for sensitive skin. However, for individuals with extreme sensitivities, we recommend opting for our solid gold or stainless steel pieces for the highest level of comfort. If you have specific allergies or concerns, feel free to reach out, and we’ll be happy to assist you in finding the perfect piece!
